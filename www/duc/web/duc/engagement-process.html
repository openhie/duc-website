    How to Engage

    Share: For those who want to have their voices heard, there is an opportunity to share your experience and knowledge.

    • Share your experience on a particular topic like interruption in treatment on a one-on-one call with our team
    • Have access to community notes
    • Have the opportunity to increase participation at any time

    Share, Learn, and Help Design Solutions: For those who want to participate in a monthly or semi-monthly call that enables you to share your experience, learn from the experience of others and provide key input on possible solutions.

    • Share your experience on a particular topic like interruption in treatment on a one-on-one call with our team
    • Listen to others share on the same topic
    • Have access to community notes
    • Engage in determining ideas and solutions

    Share, Learn, and Try Out Solutions: For those who are looking to try out solutions and share feedback.

    • Share your experience on a particular topic like interruption in treatment on a one-on-one call with our team
    • Listen to others share on the same topic
    • Have access to community notes
    • Engage in determining possible ideas or solutions
    • Try solutions and share results and ideas for making the solutions better
