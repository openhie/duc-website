    • The Data Use Community

    The Data Use Community (DUC) is an open community of organizations and individuals passionate about improving health and healthcare data sharing. This community is set on a mission to address three key needs:

    Best Practices
    Practitioners can share on which interventions, tools, and processes are working
    Donor Alignment
    Donors will have the opportunity to align investments and strategies
    In-Country Perspective
    Everyone understands the real in-country needs and challenges

    JOIN THE DISCUSSION

    See recent discussions within the community on DUC's Discourse platform. We welcome you to contribute and interact!

    DUC COMMUNITY OVERVIEW

    The DUC is focused on bringing together diverse perspectives and voices. We will continue to engage in this process to develop the necessary outputs and outcomes to create a sustainable impact.
